Personality Test Results



According to keirsey.com, based off the short quiz that I participated in, my temperament results were stated to be Guardian. People with this type of temperament are said to be humble, enthusiastic and hardworking. These people focus on getting the job done and making sure it is done as best as practicable. These people are often quiet and like to be around people who they are familiar with. This test seems valid to me because the guardian personality type is totally me, could not be more accurate. I would highly recommend this assessment tool.
